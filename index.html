Ruben Wiersma

I'm a Research Scientist at Adobe Research in Paris. My research topics include computer graphics, geometry processing and machine learning.

Previously, I received my PhD in Computer Science (Cum Laude, Eurographics PhD Award) from the CGV Group at TU Delft, supervised by Elmar Eisemann and Klaus Hildebrandt, and was a postdoc at the Interactive Geometry Lab at ETH Zurich, advised by Olga Sorkine-Hornung. Outside of research, I like to work on short films, graphic design and making music.
