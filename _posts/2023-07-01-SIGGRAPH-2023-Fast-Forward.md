To introduce [Gravo MG](https://rubenwiersma.nl/gravomg) at the SIGGRAPH 2023 Fast Forward, I chose to communicate a simple message: speed. In the 20-second teaser video, we show how many solves our method could perform (15), while other approaches solved none.

SIGGRAPH is the principal conference for computer graphics worldwide. In the Fast Forward session, attendees get a quick, often humorous, and high-energy overview of all the works that are presented at the conference.
