For the SIGGRAPH 2025 Fast Forward, I wanted to do something light, but with high polish. [Our paper](https://svbrdf-uncertainty.github.io) was about estimating uncertainty for material capture, so I quickly made the association with a therapy session. I cast my colleague (to remain anonymous) as the 'material', dressed in a metallic suit and presented live in-costume (imagine standing in a morph suit in front of 2000 people, lots of fun).
