I am a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zürich in the Interactive Geometry Lab (IGL). My interests include geometry processing, optimization, and machine learning. I received my doctorate cum laude from the CGV group at the TU Delft and interned at Adobe as a research intern, studying material capture with differentiable rendering. I have a soft spot for working with creatives and enjoy working on my own short films, design and music.
